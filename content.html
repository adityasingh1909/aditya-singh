CAME FROM REDDIT




1. THE PUPPY IN THE BASEMENT

This short ghost story, found on Reddit, is super creepy:

“Mommy told me never to go in the basement, but I wanted to see what was making that noise. It kind of sounded like a puppy, and I wanted to see the puppy, so I opened the basement door and tiptoed down a bit. I didn’t see a puppy, and then Mommy yanked me out of the basement and yelled at me. Mommy had never yelled at me before, and it made me sad and I cried. Then Mommy told me never to go into the basement again, and she gave me a cookie. That made me feel better, so I didn’t ask her why the boy in the basement was making noises like a puppy, or why he had no hands or feet.”

2. THERE'S SOMEONE UNDER THE BED

Sometimes the very short ghost stories are the best, and this tale, from Reddit via Buzzfeed, tells the story of a father putting his young son to bed:

“I begin tucking him into bed and he tells me, “Daddy, check for monsters under my bed.” I look underneath for his amusement and see him, another him, under the bed, staring back at me quivering and whispering, “Daddy, there’s somebody on my bed."

3. THE LOUD BANGING

this one from Reddit will raise your hair:

I was 8 or 9 year old when this happened. Every night around 2am I used to hear a hammering sound coming from the first floor. For context I live in a house which have three floors with a roof and the hammering sounds always came from the first floor, I always thought my uncle (my father's brother) may be doing something, because he always complained about how he couldn't sleep at night. I thought he might have found a new hobby or something, but no one else ever complained about that noise or even mentioned it. Anyways My bedroom was on third floor and it had a balcony with a wooden door. One night my father was out of city for some work and all of my other family members were asleep so I decided to stay up all night and watch TV, but I feel asleep. I woke up to a loud banging sound coming from the balcony door, I immediately got up and asked in a voice that I am not scared "who is outside" and the banging got louder. I screamed very loudly and my mom came up to my and asked why I was screaming? I was crying so she hugged me and asked again, as I was trying to tell her about what have happened, the banging on the door started again and it was so brutal this time that I thought it might broke that door. My mother called my father's friend who was a police officer and informed my father as well. After my father's friend came, he checked the balcony and there was nothing there, he even asked us to check it with him so we wouldn't be afraid of our own house. After that nothing like that ever happened again and the hammering sound well never heard that again too. I never asked my family about that hammering sound because I am still afraid that I was the only one that was hearing it.



4. PEEKABOO I SEE YOU

Came from Reddit, this one's gonna baffle you brains:

When my daughter was born I would be the one to primarily put her to bed in her crib at night, I would rock her back and forth in an old chair in the corner of the room. One night while I was rocking her to sleep, she perked up and started waving at the wall in the room, it was weird but children do weird things so I brushed it off. She would do weird things like this every other night. However the creepest moment was one night when she was sleeping in the bed with my wife and I and we (me and my daughter) both woke up at the same time in the middle of the night. I saw that the door to our bedroom was wide open and my daughter propped herself up giggled and covered her eyes saying "peekaboo I see you" into the darkness. This would be creepy enough on its own but my daughter was really young and couldnt say very many phrases at the time, and this was the first time she ever said those words. Needless to say it was an uneasy night

5. IT WANTED ME ALONE IN THE GARAGE

Came from Reddit, do you have an eerie garage too?

A lot of things went down in this house. But out of all the spooky things they’ve happened to me there, this one just crossed my mind after 18 years. I was 14 years old living in Orange County with my 2 sisters and my mother. We had a rough upbringing so renting an actually house was like a dream come true for us at that time. Until we realized we were living in a haunted house. I was with my mom the day we rented. The garage was scary as hell even in the daylight with people around, it just always gave me a clustered feeling of something being there I couldn’t see. When the garage door was shut it was pitch black, no light at all from outside could get through that garage door. During the summer I would wake up early, I would listen to my cd’s on my radio (so every time I came in or out I made sure my bedroom door was shut because my mom liked to sleep in the living on the couch right outside my bedroom). I would clean my room, the kitchen and bathroom before I would ask my mom if I could meet my best friend at our halfway mark. This particular morning the funky spooky things of the house started early. As I walked out of my bedroom, shut my door walked down the hall passing two bedroom to the restroom I noticed something from the corner of my eye in the kitchen, like a silhouette of a person that vanished (this was normal).
My moms bedroom was directly outside the bathroom doorway to the right and it had a private bathroom which lead to the garage. Well as I was walking out the restroom I seen a person walking into my moms bedroom, like a women (which I assumed was my mother since we were the only ones home). Thinking it was my mom I was talking to her about leaving to my friends and the bedroom door shut on my face and I was think “wow she’s rude today”. So I opened the door and again asked if I could go to my friends when her bathroom door started shutting.. when I opened the bathroom door and said “mom!” I realized the garage door was wide open (the garage door was heavy and did not stay open on its own ever) and it was pitch black. In that moment I knew it wasn’t my mom so I ran out, shut her bedroom door behind me started toward my bedroom when I noticed my bedroom door was wide open. I was standing in my room confused and realized my radio was off . I tried turning it in multiple times and seen it was actually unplugged. I went to the living room to wake my mom up and ask if she had went into my room to unplug my radio she said she didn’t ever go into my room or touch my radio that morning because she was sleeping still. I don’t even know why I had to ask if she went into the garage through her bedroom for clarification… but needless to say it wasn’t her either. She asked me t please not leave her there alone, but I did and I feel bad still. Anyways, that night I didn’t want to come back home. I know whatever it was this time, it wanted me in that garage alone.
